Ducati Panigale V4

The Panigale V4 MY23 represents the last step in the characteristic path of Borgo Panigale's sports bikes. As is the case with motorcycles engaged in MotoGP and WorldSBK, the Panigale V4 is updated and improved year after year, and the 2022 version represents the most significant evolution since its launch year. For MY2023, a series of electronic improvements have been introduced that make the bike even easier and more intuitive for riders of all levels.

Ducati Streetfighter V4

The Streetfighter V4 was a massive hit in the super sports naked motorcycle segment, thanks to its aggressive and minimalist design, inspired by the Joker from comics. It quickly became a favorite among enthusiasts. Its evolution takes the "Fight Formula" to the next level, with elements like a Panigale V4 without fairings, high and wide handlebars, a lightweight 178 kg frame, a powerful 208 hp Desmosedici Stradale engine, biplane wings for aerodynamics, and cutting-edge electronic features.
